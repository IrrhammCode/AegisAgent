HOW IT WORKS

Three Steps to Permanent Protection

Aegis protects your data in three simple, fully autonomous stages.

1

Detect

The agent scans your files and uses Impulse AI to classify sensitive data — passwords, financial records, personal information — all automatically.

2

Prove

A Zero-Knowledge proof is generated using Noir. This cryptographically proves your data is authentic and untampered — without ever revealing its contents.

3

Archive

Your proven data is permanently stored on Arweave via Irys. It can never be deleted, altered, or censored — guaranteed for 200+ years.
